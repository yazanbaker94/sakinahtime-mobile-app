# SakinahTime App Improvement Plan

## Current App Overview

SakinahTime is a comprehensive Islamic app with:
- **Prayer Times** - Location-based prayer times with notifications and azan
- **Qibla Compass** - Direction finder with haptic feedback
- **Mushaf (Quran)** - Full Quran with tafsir, translations, audio, bookmarks, search, highlights, notes
- **Azkar** - Morning/evening/prayer remembrances with tap-to-count tasbeeh
- **Islamic Guides** - How-to guides for worship, hajj, fasting, etc.
- **99 Names of Allah** - Asmaul Husna with meanings

✅ **Already Has:** Tasbeeh counter integrated in Azkar (tap to count, reset, target tracking)

---

## HIGH PRIORITY IMPROVEMENTS

### 1. Quran Reading Progress & Khatm Tracker
**Why:** Users want to track their Quran completion journey
- Daily reading goals (pages/verses per day)
- Progress bar showing completion percentage
- Khatm (completion) counter
- Reading streaks and statistics
- Reminder notifications for daily reading
-COMPLETED.

### 2. Hijri Calendar Integration
**Why:** Muslims need Islamic dates for fasting, events
- Display current Hijri date prominently
- Islamic events calendar (Ramadan, Eid, Ashura, etc.)
- Countdown to upcoming events
- Moon phase indicator
- Fasting day reminders (Mondays, Thursdays, White Days)

### 3. Offline Mode Enhancement
**Why:** Users need the app to work without internet
- Download Quran audio for offline playback
- Cache prayer times for 30 days ahead
- Offline tafsir access
- Clear storage management UI

## MEDIUM PRIORITY IMPROVEMENTS

### 4. Widget Support
**Why:** Quick access without opening app
- Prayer times widget (next prayer countdown)
- Daily verse widget
- Tasbeeh counter widget
- Hijri date widget

### 5. Quran Memorization Mode (Hifz)
**Why:** Many users are memorizing Quran
- Hide/reveal text for testing
- Repeat verse/page X times
- Mark memorized sections
- Revision schedule suggestions
- Audio loop for specific verses

### 6. Dua Collection
**Why:** Expand beyond azkar to comprehensive duas
- Categorized duas (travel, eating, sleeping, etc.)
- Quranic duas with references
- Prophetic duas from hadith
- Favorites and custom duas
- Audio pronunciation

### 7. Prayer Log & Statistics
**Why:** Help users track their prayer consistency
- Mark prayers as prayed/missed
- Weekly/monthly prayer statistics
- Streak tracking
- Gentle reminders for missed prayers
- Qada (makeup) prayer tracker

### 8. Ramadan Mode
**Why:** Special features during the holy month
- Suhoor/Iftar times with notifications
- Daily Quran reading schedule for completion
- Taraweeh prayer tracker
- Charity (Sadaqah) tracker
- Laylatul Qadr countdown

### 9. Community Features
**Why:** Social engagement increases retention
- Share verses/duas to social media
- Daily verse notifications
- Leaderboards for reading (optional)
- Group Quran reading challenges
